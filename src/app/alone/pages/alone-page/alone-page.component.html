About My Maps App

Welcome to the "Maps App"! This application is designed to showcase various mapping features, including fullscreen maps, markers, properties, zoom controls, and more.

The app was built using Angular, leveraging features such as standalone components and lazy loading for better performance and scalability.

I developed this app to learn and experiment with mapping tools, responsive design, and advanced Angular features. It's a testament to my skills in modern web development and my passion for creating intuitive and powerful applications.

Feel free to explore the different sections of the app using the navigation menu. Thank you for visiting!
